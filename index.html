Future of Insurance Today

About This Webinar

Future of Insurance Today will look at how innovation, collaboration and cloud allow the insurance sector to change and improve across three key areas: Insurance Processes, Security and Compliance, Customer Interactions and Communications. Microsoft, with their strategic SI (Systems Integrator) and ISV (Independent SoftwareVendor) partners Realdolmen (Traviata), Solgari and Cloud 2020, will show a modern insurance solution when it is presented as one easy to use interface inside Microsoft Dynamics 365.

Our webinar will focus on three key benefits to insurance companies

Operational Efficiency

If all insurance processes, customer data and communications are held securely in the cloud they become accessible from anywhere. This facilitates the modern flexible working environments we now all need such as working remotely without compromising on security, recording information or communication options.

Enhanced Data, Security & Compliance

When all your customer data becomes easily accessible in one, highly secure platform maintaining compliance for regulations such as GDPR and MIFID II is not compromised by a change in your ways of working. We'll also show you how moving to a Dynamics 365 platform helps companies to achieve PCI DSS compliance and more.

Improved Agent & Customer Experience

This solution will show you the power of a collaborative approach to insurance demonstrating the access all the customer data and the communications capabilities inside an insurance specific interface. Making sure that you build trust with your customers by offering them a seamless experience across multiple channels.
